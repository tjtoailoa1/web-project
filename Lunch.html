Lunch

Recipes

Ingredients

    1.50 g Butter, softened 2.8 Toast bread slices, thick 3.4+ slices of cheese 4.4+ slices of ham(or another meat of your choose)

    Directions

    1.Butter one side of the bread slices generously. 2.Top 4 slices with the ham and cheeses on the unbuttered side, then sandwich with remaining slices. 3.Preheat a non stick pan and sear sandwich for 3-4 minutes each side over a gentle heat until well toasted and cheese begins to ooze.
