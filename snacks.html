Kiwi Dip


Ingredients


    1. 250g Can Reduced Cream
    2. 1 pkt Onion Soup Mix
    3. 1 tsp Fresh Lemon Juice
    4. Items To Dip, eg: Fresh Vegetables, Rice Crackers, Pita Bread, Chips

    Directions


    1. Place reduced cream, onion soup mix and lemon juice in a bowl and mix well to combine.
    2. Cover and chill for about 30 minutes before serving.

Rice Krispy Treats


Ingredients


    1. Cooking spray
    2. 3 tablespoons butter
    3. 1 bag Jet-Puffed minimarshmallows
    4. 6 cups cereal of your choice

    Directions


    1. Lightly grease a spatula and a standard casserole dish.
    2. In large nonstick saucepan, melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows, and stir occasionally until completely melted. Remove from heat.
    3. Add cereal. Quickly stir until cereal is well coated.
    4. Pour and press into casserole dish until evenly distributed. Cool for about 30 minutes. Cut into squares.

Chocolate Chip Cookie


Ingredients


    1. ​125g butter, softened
    2. ¼ cup soft brown sugar
    3. ½ cup white Sugar
    4. ½ tsp vanilla essence
    5. 1 egg
    6. 1¾ cups self-raising flour
    7. ½ tsp salt
    8. 1 cup chocolate chunks or chips

    Directions


    1. Preheat oven to 180°C conventional or 160ºC fan forced and grease or line two baking trays with baking paper.
    2. Beat butter, Chelsea Soft Brown Sugar, Chelsea Caster Sugar and vanilla until pale and creamy, add egg and beat until mixed.
    3. Sift in flour and salt, mix until combined before stirring in the chocolate chunks or chips.
    4. Roll heaped teaspoon sized balls of mixture and place on tray, press down lightly with a fork. Bake for approx. 12 minutes.

Coconut Bread


Ingredients


    1. 2 Eggs
    2. 453g Coconut Milk
    3. 2 tsp Vanilla Essence
    4. 2¼ cups plain flour
    5. 2 tsp baking powder
    6. 1½ cups dextrose
    7. 1¼ cups of desiccated coconut
    8. 75g unsalted butter

    Directions


    1.Preheat Oven to 160°C and lightly grease about 10 muffin tins
    2. Whisk eggs. coconut milk and vanilla in a bowl
    3. Sift flour, baking powder in a large bowl, add dextrose and coconut and stir to combine. Then add coconut milk mixture and the butter and stir until its all mixed in
    4. Spoon Batter into the perpared muffin tins and sprinkle with a bit more desiccated coconut
    5. Bake the breads for 20 to 30 minutes or until tested with a skewer (they should be pale) leave to cool for 5 minutes in tins the transfer to cooling rack
